Welcome to the F-117 Nighthawk - Angular Devkit package. This is a production-ready, enterprise-grade project developed with Bootstrap, offering extensive custom functionality. If you are an Angular developer, you are likely familiar with ng-bootstrap, ngx-bootstrap, and other Angular/Bootstrap packages, which you may have already used. This project, initially created for in-house development by the skilled Angular team at EVENUX, has expanded in both features and scope. In the beginning, it used to be a paid package, but we have decided to make it available as a FREE package that can be utilized in a wide range of projects.

At EVENUX, we possess extensive experience in Angular development, leveraging one of the most versatile and refined frameworks available today. Our objective is to provide a comprehensive foundation for your projects, offering pre-built components for all your needs. Simply configure the SASS style variables, and you will be ready to bring your application to life exactly as you envision it.
